About the Legal AI Assistant

This application is a **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system** designed to help you analyze and understand legal documents. It combines a Large Language Model (LLM) with your own private data to provide context-aware, accurate, and relevant answers.

Core RAG Workflow

1. Upload a Document

2. Chunking & Embedding

3. Store in ChromaDB

4. Ask a Question

5. Semantic Search & Context

6. Generate Response

Upload your legal documents in **PDF** or **DOCX** format. This is the foundation of the RAG system, allowing the AI to be grounded in your specific data.

Once a document is uploaded, you can ask questions about its content. The AI assistant uses a **semantic search** to find the most relevant information from your document and uses that as context to provide a concise and accurate answer. This prevents the AI from "hallucinating" and ensures its responses are directly from your provided text.
